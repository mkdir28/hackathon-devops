# CV career planning prompt (template)

Use with your CV/resume pasted or uploaded. Replace all `{{PLACEHOLDERS}}` before sending to an LLM.

---

I'm a {{YEAR}} at {{SCHOOL}} targeting these roles:

• {{TARGET_ROLE_1}} at a {{COMPANY_TYPE_1}} — {{CAREERS_URL_1}}

• {{TARGET_ROLE_2}} at a {{COMPANY_TYPE_2}} — {{CAREERS_URL_2}}

• {{TARGET_ROLE_3}} at a {{COMPANY_TYPE_3}} — {{CAREERS_URL_3}}

**My background (summary):**
- Major / focus: {{DEGREE_OR_FOCUS}}
- Years of experience: {{YEARS_EXPERIENCE}} (include internships, projects, freelance)
- Location preference: {{CITY}}, {{COUNTRY}} ({{WORK_MODE}} — remote / hybrid / onsite)
- Target start: {{TARGET_START_DATE}}

**Attached:** my resume/CV (see upload or paste below).

---

After analyzing my resume against these roles, build:

## 1. Action Tracker (Google Doc)

Forward-looking planner showing what to do next. Include:

- **Skill gaps** — ranked by impact for each target role, with specific projects to build (name, scope, 2–4 week deliverable, how it proves the skill)
- **Timeline** — semester / year / graduation milestones through {{GRADUATION_DATE}}
- **Networking targets** — {{NUM_NETWORKING_TARGETS}} people or communities (alumni, meetups, LinkedIn groups, conferences) with why each matters
- **Learning roadmap** — courses, certs, books, labs (prioritized, with estimated hours)
- **Local resources** — career center, clubs, hackathons, job boards relevant to {{CITY}} / {{COUNTRY}}

Format: tight spacing, scannable headers, built for weekly planning. One section per target role plus a shared “this month” checklist.

## 2. Skills Portfolio Log (Excel)

Backward-looking evidence bank showing what I've done. Include columns/sheets for:

- **Projects log** — project name, dates, role, stack, metrics (use {{METRIC_PLACEHOLDER}} if unknown, e.g. “reduced latency by X%”), link to repo/demo
- **Skills inventory** — skill, proficiency (1–5), proof (project, job, cert), last used
- **Quantified achievements** — bullet, number, context, competency tag
- **Learning completions** — course/cert, date, hours, key takeaway
- **STAR stories** — Situation, Task, Action, Result; tagged by competency (leadership, technical, communication, etc.)

Built for mining resume bullets and cover letters. Flag gaps where I have no proof yet.

## 3. Role fit summary

For each target role, provide:

- Match score (0–100) and top 3 strengths
- Top 3 gaps and fastest fix (≤ 30 days each)
- Suggested resume headline and 2-line summary tailored to that role
- 3 interview stories to prepare
